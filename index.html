Mechatronics Engineering & Mechanical Reliability

Practical engineering, designed for purpose

I’m a mechatronics engineer and fitter. My philosophy is simple: engineering should fit the environment, not the other way around. Over-engineering can be just as problematic as under-engineering.

I focus on designing hardware that is fit-for-purpose. If your project needs to withstand high-pressure washdowns or extreme vibration, I have the experience to hit standards like IP69K and MIL-STD-810H. But I also know when those standards are overkill, and when a simpler, more maintainable design is the smarter choice for your bottom line.
