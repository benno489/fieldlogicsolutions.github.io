About the Founder

Field Logic Solutions is led by Ben, a Mechatronics Engineer and Dual-Trade Fitter/Machinist. His experience spans over two decades of heavy industrial work, ranging from wind turbine construction, underground mining, and mobile plant operations to precision CNC machining, electronics housing design, and vibration testing.

This "Dual-Trade" background—the intersection of formal engineering theory and intensive workshop floor experience—is the foundation of our firm. It ensures that every project we undertake is governed by a practical understanding of how assets fail in the real world.
