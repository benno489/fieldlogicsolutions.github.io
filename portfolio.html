Mechatronic Design Stream

Harsh-Environment Telemetry Enclosure

The Challenge

Mitigating catastrophic field failure patterns caused by transient harmonic vibration, moisture ingress, and thermal shifting on custom sensor arrays deployed across extreme agricultural environments.

The Execution

Architected a custom, high-durability housing leveraging advanced, high-temperature industrial ASA printing frameworks. Designed tight-tolerance internal structural walls combined with custom-machined mechanical interfaces to mechanically isolate microcontrollers from physical chassis shocks.

The Outcome

Delivered completely sealed, field-ready hardware blocks achieving zero system structural failures across intense mechanical implementation evaluations, locking in data logging integrity.

High-Speed Telemetry Waveform Analysis

Forensic Diagnostics Stream

Transient Hydraulic & CAN Bus Fault Isolation

The Challenge

Isolating high-frequency, intermittent component shut-downs on high-pressure fluid power machinery loops where standard hand-held service diagnostic tools failed to log any fault markers.

The Execution

Utilised multi-channel automotive oscilloscopes (Picoscope telemetry) coupled with high-speed digital logging architecture directly inline with the physical CAN network. Captured data spikes inside micro-second frames to trap hidden system-wide drops.

The Outcome

Pinpointed a transient micro-solenoid earthing fault occurring only under full operational heat loads, generating an engineering roadmap to eliminate recurring system downtime.
