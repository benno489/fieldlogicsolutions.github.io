01 // Systems Optimisation

Harsh-Environment Telemetry Enclosure

The Challenge:

Mitigating high-frequency harmonic vibration and dust ingress on sensor arrays deployed across un-airconditioned, corrugated paddock operations.

The Execution:

Engineered a custom IP67 structural housing utilizing high-strength, UV-stable additive manufacturing (ASA). Integrated custom-lathed mechanical dampening mounts to isolate sensitive electronic solder joints.

The Outcome:

Zero systemic structural failures recorded across multi-fleet field trials, protecting data logging continuity.

02 // Forensic Analysis

Transient Hydraulic Fault Isolation

The Challenge:

Isolating intermittent, invisible root-cause component dropouts in high-pressure fluid power systems that consistently bypassed standard field multi-meters.

The Execution:

Deployed high-fidelity waveform telemetry and diagnostic sensor capture arrays to log micro-second voltage and pressure spikes directly in the field loop.

The Outcome:

Successfully located a transient micro-solenoid earthing fault, preventing a total asset rebuild and saving substantial unscheduled downtime.
